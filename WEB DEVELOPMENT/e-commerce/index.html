Subsidy Removal: Shettima, 36 Govs In NEC Meeting

This is second meeting of the Council and it is expected to deliberate on the reviewed palliative package for Nigerians following petrol subsidy removal.

The meeting, which is being chaired by the Vice President Kashim Shettima, has in attendance governors of the 36 states of the Federation, the Director General of the Nigerian Governors’ Forum, as well as stakeholders from the World Bank and other agencies of governmen
